About Us

Villa Alleira Seminyak is a renovated five-bedroom family villa, located in the heart of famous Seminyak, within easy walking distance to Ku-De-Ta beach. The property offers a spacious, open-air living area adjoined with a dining space for 8, and includes a well-equipped kitchen. Outside features green surrounding a private pool with poolside loungers and a BBQ grill. The property includes a large balcony overlooking the swimming pool, the perfect setting for private dinner parties or gathering with friends. When staying at our villa, you will enjoy a variety of relaxing spaces with a complete privacy. You can relax poolside, request in-villa spa and massage services, enjoy your personal staff and more exclusive services from our Professional team.
